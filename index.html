My Favorite Restaurants

Kristoffer Laquian - Week 3, Assigment 1

These are my Top 3 Restaurants in Saipan. All of which have their own reasons, but are in no particular order.

Restaurants

  1. The Hut
  2. J's Restaurant
  3. Sunny Side

The Hut

The Hut is mainly focused on Mexican Cuisine. However, they do it very well! These are my top 3 dishes from there.

  • California Fries
  • Liberation Burrito
  • Rice Plate

J's Restaurant

J's Restauant, I would say is a good mix of Filipino and Local cuisines.
Their service usually takes a while, but the food does not disappoint!

  • Cheese Burger
  • Bulalo(Beef Bone Marrow Stew)
  • Tocino w/Egg and Fried Rice

Sunny Side Cafe

In my opinion, this is the best Filipino Restauant in Saipan! Their service is very good, and hospitiality is on point!
